Our Story

Degree32 was born from frustration. As engineers working on climate modeling software, we kept encountering the same problem: floating-point arithmetic errors in temperature conversions were introducing noise into our datasets.

The fix seemed simple enough—use fixed-point decimal arithmetic—but implementing it correctly, at scale, with proper rounding strategies, turned out to be surprisingly complex. We built the solution we needed, and realized others needed it too.

Today, Degree32 powers temperature conversions for thousands of applications, from weather apps to industrial control systems to scientific research platforms.
